# ❤️ Heart Disease Data Visualization

This project focuses on exploring a heart disease dataset to understand the key factors that influence heart-related conditions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze patient data and identify patterns, relationships, and risk factors associated with heart disease using visualization techniques.

---

## 📊 Dataset

* Contains medical attributes such as:

  * Age
  * Sex
  * Chest pain type
  * Blood pressure
  * Cholesterol
  * Heart disease presence

---

## ❓ Business / Health Questions

* Does age increase the risk of heart disease?
* Are males more affected than females?
* Which medical factors are strongly correlated with heart disease?
* Which features are most important for prediction?

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

### 1. Data Overview

* Checked structure and basic information
* Identified important features

### 2. Age Analysis

* Studied distribution of age
* Observed how age relates to disease

### 3. Categorical Analysis

* Compared gender, chest pain, and other categories

### 4. Correlation Analysis

* Used heatmap to find relationships between variables

### 5. Advanced Visualizations

* Pairplots, jointplots, and violin plots
* Deeper understanding of feature interactions

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Age plays an important role in heart disease risk
* Some features show strong correlation with disease
* Certain categories (like chest pain type) are highly informative
* Visualization helps reveal hidden patterns
